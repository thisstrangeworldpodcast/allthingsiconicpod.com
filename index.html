Episodes

All Things Iconic is your one stop destination for today’s hot topics. Each week, Jenna Naphtali takes you through the world of pop culture and well, whatever else is on her mind. Stay tuned for special guests, fun games, and a whole lot of laughs. Be sure to listen every Tuesday, it’s going to be iconic.

  • Dec 21, 2021

    A Kardashian Kristmas - Season 1 Episode 6

    As a Jew, Christmas for Jenna means Chinese food and stalking the Kardashians’ social media. It’s a known fact (or a Jenna fact) that nobody does Christmas better than the Kardashians. Jenna brings on her friend Sydney to discuss the glamorous Kardashians and admire the elaborate ways they celebrate Christmas. Bible, it’s a great episode.

  • Dec 14, 2021

    And Just Like That…Is Sex and the City back? - Season 1 Episode 5

    The long awaited Sex and the City reboot is here! Jenna brings on her friend, Zawadi, and while they might not have been the original target audience for the show, they sure do have some thoughts. They couldn’t help but wonder… was this reboot a flop?

  • Dec 7, 2021

    Bachelor Nation Tea - Season 1 Episode 4

    The tea is brewing in Bachelor Nation! This week, Jenna enlists her friend and Bachelor expert, Dante to help her spill it all. Tune in as these two discuss the not so rosy breakups, scandals, and much more!

  • Nov 30, 2021

    Are You Cheugy? - Season 1 Episode 3

    Within every extended family, people have their differences, but are those differences generational or environmental? Jenna gives a glimpse into her crazy family as she and her “Zillennial” twin sister go head to head against their two millennial cousins.

  • Nov 23, 2021

    A Friendsgiving Special - Season 1 Episode 2

    With Thanksgiving around the corner, Jenna brings on her best friend from day 1 of college, Gerald, to celebrate Friendsgiving. They catch up like good friends do and share what in pop culture they are must thankful for. To close things off they play a game of “Pass The Turkey”… you’ll have to tune in to hear what it’s all about.

  • Nov 16, 2021

    Weird Celebrity Couples - Season 1 Episode 1

    In Hollywood, you have the “it” couples, the divorcees, and the playboys, but what about those random celebrity couples? Maybe you forgot they dated, or maybe you never knew. This week, Jenna brings on her friend Tatum to delve into the weirdest, most random celebrity couples from the past. They then create some of their own random celebrity pairings, because why not??
